# Revenue Recognition Policy

Revenue is recognized when control of goods or services transfers to the
customer. This policy governs how the finance organization books revenue across
subscription, usage-based and one-time contracts.

Related guidance lives in [[quarterly-close]] and the [Billing Glossary](billing-glossary.md).
